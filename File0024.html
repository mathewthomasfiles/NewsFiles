Five more cases of Aadhaar-related frauds at two PSBs

Indian Overseas Bank had the maximum of 15 such cases wherein Rs 5.89 lakh were siphoned off, followed by State Bank of India.

Written by Sunny Verma
New Delhi | Updated: January 5, 2018 09:23 IST
Andhra Bank witnessed four such cases wherein Rs 4,20,098 was withdrawn from customers accounts using their Aadhaar details without their knowledge.

Five recent cases of money being fraudulently withdrawn from customers bank accounts have come to light at two public sector banks, where the fraud was perpetrated using the customers Aadhaar number linked with the accounts.
